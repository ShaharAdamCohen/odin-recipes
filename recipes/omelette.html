Omelette

Description

This recipe is all about Omelettes and how to make it in the best way possible.

We will go over the ingredients and steps of preparation.

Ingredients:

  • Large eggs - 2
  • Salt - A pinch
  • Butter - About 30 grams
  • Chives - A small bunch

Steps:

For best result make sure the eggs are room temperature

  1. Melt butter on non-stick pan under medium heat
  2. Crack the eggs into a small bowl and whisk thoroughly
  3. Pour eggs into pan
  4. Create shape
  5. Put chives on finished omelette
